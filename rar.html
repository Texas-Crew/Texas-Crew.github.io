RENT-A-ROWER

Rent-A-Rower is a fundraising service provided by Texas Crew to support the students and faculty of The University of Texas and the community of Austin. Simply rent-a-rower to help you with tasks involving manual labor, yard work, babysitting, tutoring, moving, technical support, and more! Our rowers are strong, dependable, and reliable. As well as being strong, our rowers are smart, earning degrees spanning from Chemical Engineering, Computer Science, and Finance to French and Art History.

Please complete the submission form below and we will work with your schedule. The fee to rent-a-rower is $13/hr per rower. Your donation will help lower team dues, enter races, and purchase new equipment. Thank you for your support and we look forward to serving you.
